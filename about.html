About Us

Meet Team 1912 Combustion - where passion for robotics meets the future of STEM leadership.

Who Are We?

We are a passionate group of high school students and dedicated adult mentors, both technical and non-technical, united by a mission:

To inspire and develop the next generation of STEM leaders in our school, community, and region through robotics competition, outreach, and community service.

What's Our Purpose?

Our goal is to inspire students and make our schools and community proud by building competitive robots that excel in design, fabrication, programming, and performance.

  • Build student technical skills and knowledge
  • Continuously improve our design, fabrication, and programming techniques
  • Keep our sponsors and supporters informed about our work and successes

About the Team

Team 1912 Combustion is the official FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team representing Northshore and Salmen High Schools in Slidell, Louisiana.

2005

Founded

by a small group of Northshore seniors

2014

Growth

Welcomed members of former FRC Team 4668 from Salmen High

Today

Present

A diverse family of driven students and mentoring professionals

But for us, building the robot is only the beginning.

We stay active throughout the year with community demos, outreach events, volunteer efforts, and STEM camps, spreading our passion for innovation across the region.
