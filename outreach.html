Community Outreach

Team 1912 is committed to inspiring the next generation of STEM leaders through community engagement, educational initiatives, and promoting robotics and technology throughout our community.

STEM Education

We organize workshops, demonstrations, and hands-on activities to introduce students to robotics, programming, and engineering principles.

Mentorship Programs

Our team members mentor FLL and FTC teams, helping younger students develop their STEM skills and fostering their interest in robotics.

Community Events

We participate in local events, fairs, and demonstrations to share our passion for robotics and inspire others to get involved in STEM.
